Know More About Infigo Centres

Company

Who we are

Infigo Eyecare has its genesis stemming from a single-minded aspiration - to provide comprehensive eye care solutions that match international standards. Our team of highly qualified and skilled doctors are dedicated in providing the best in safety, quality, and value to meet our patrons’ needs. Our facilities are equipped with the latest German and American technology, and rank amongst the best in the world. They are regularly calibrated for the most accurate diagnosis and readings. You will find our highly qualified doctors and technicians are team-centric, patient-focused and solution-driven. Since our inception in 2018, we have already served almost 40,000 patients with the support of our 18 super specialty doctors and 200 support staff.

Purpose

Good eye specialists in India usually are associated with some hospital or have a practice of their own. There too, they perform mostly as generalists in the realm of eye care. Their own core specialty, often are under-utilized, and never quite reach the patients the way it should.

Our purpose is to spread the awareness of the super specialties available in the space of ophthalmology and to ensure everyone has access to it.

Differentiator

Backed by cutting edge technology and bio-science innovations in the hands of super qualified doctors who are committedly employed to provide you the best of ophthalmology care across 35* specialties, Infigo Eyecare is the first of its kind comprehensive eye hospital chain with multiple specialties under one roof.

Values

Our uncompromising approach to eye care, underpinned by integrity and excellence, assures you that your eyes are in the most competent hands.

Vision

Create international benchmarks in eye care, not just follow them, and be recognized as India’s leading and most trusted eye care institution that offers uncompromising eye care to everyone who needs it – from pre-born and infants to adults and seniors while upholding the ethical path, at all times.

Mission

To create a robust, comprehensive eye care infrastructure that provides world-class, timely treatment using cutting edge technology, service innovation and skillful talent at our warm and patient-friendly facilities across the country.

Philosophy

Truly revolutionize and democratize eye care for all. By empowering people with knowledge about the various ailments that can affect their eyes by giving access to the best of doctors qualified to diagnose and discuss treatments for an informed choice.
